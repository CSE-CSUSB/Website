Upcoming Events:

General Club Meeting

Friday, January 26, 2018 @ 2 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 359

Join us as we discuss future events, showcase student projects, eat pizza, and network. This is the time to get your questions answered about IEEE, how to join the club, and how to get involved and start volunteering.

Debug the World

Friday, January 26, 2018 @ 3:30 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 359

There you are, working on your programming homework the night before it's due. But, the moment of truth comes when you compile that file, cross your fingers, then BAM, “Segmentation Fault.” Fear no more, as we have a workshop that will help you debug your program, pinpoint the source of your trouble, and get that project done.

“Git Fu” Workshop

Friday, February 2, 2018 @ 3:30 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 359

Are you finding it difficult to maintain your project team’s code over the quarter? Git is the version control system you are looking for: easy to learn, easy to take control. Master the art of using Git in our Git workshop. Soon, you can keep track of your software like a pro!

IDE Workshop

Friday, February 9, 2018 @ 3:30 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 359

Are you having a difficult time using a text editor to write your c++ code and want to use something more powerful? IDEs are powerful tools that make coding convenient, easy to use, and allow you to discover things you may never have noticed before. This is the introduction to IDEs workshop where we showcase a handful of free C++ IDEs and what features they offer.
