“Git Fu” Workshop

Monday, November 20, 2017 @ 8-9 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 358

Are you finding it difficult to maintain your project team’s code over the quarter? Git is the version control system you are looking for: easy to learn, easy to take control. Master the art of using Git in our Git workshop on Monday, November 20th, 8-9pm. Soon, you can keep track of your software like a pro!

Study Marathon

Saturday, December 2, 2017 @ 1-5 pm

Jack Brown Hall, Third Floor, Room JB 358, 359, 360

Entry Price: $10 (pizza, soda, giveaway), members get in free

Finals week will be here before you know it! Get prepared with the CSE Club as we tackle our coursework together and have fun at the same time. This is the perfect time to get those questions answered and a second pair of eyes to look over that complicated function. Invite your friends so you can study together.
