Microsoft Academic Alliance

Are you a computer science or computer engineering student in need of tools such as Visual Studio, SQL Server, Windows Server, Visio, Windows 10/8/7 license, or other microsoft applications? If you are enrolled in a computer science course, you are eligible to obtain many Microsoft development tools free of charge for academic use. You can get these for free through the CSE deparment by filling out This Form and turning it in to room JB 351.

Opportunities and Trade-offs in Transportation: Maintaining the Region’s Competitiveness and Quality of Life

Tuesday, February 20, 2018 @ 7:30 am

SMSU Events Center B & C

The Leonard Transportation Center is hosting a series of six educational and interactive breakfast dialog meetings to bring together community leaders like you in a discussion of how to best address the region’s mobility needs. Each dialog will feature ideas and diverse opinions from well- known experts speaking on timely topics and an opportunity for you to engage with the experts and other participants. Link
