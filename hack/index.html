Winter 2019 GCP Hackathon

Days   Hrs   Mins   Secs


RSVP Here!


Saturday, March 2nd 6:00pm - 3rd 12:00pm

Library Wedge PL-1109

Ticket price: $5 for entry


Come join the Computer Science and Engineering Club as they host their first ever, Hackathon!
Students will be receiving codes worth $100 to make projects of their choice using Google Cloud Platform.

Attendees will get into teams of 3-5 people to make an application through the App Engine on Google Cloud Platform. Be warned, this is an overnight event and the challenge isn't to complete the application but to have it follow our theme! The theme will be announced on the day of the event and you will have 15 hours to hack enough code together for a demo the following morning! Members of the winning team will each receive a Google Home Mini.

Food, drinks, snacks, and most important of all, coffee, will be provided during the event to keep you all awake!
