Currently we have no active projects since it is summer! Feel free to look at our past projects, but keep in mind they are currently not in progress. Once we resume classes we will start new projects, and if you have any ideas for a project you want to start, feel free to let us know with the link below or in person in JB-357

Micromouse

What's better than watching a mouse completing a maze? Watching a minibot built by you and your very own team of students complete it!

Machine Learning

Creating life has always been a dream for inventors and philosophers everywhere. From the first automaton to IBM's Watson, the idea of imbuing a machine with the understanding of humanity's complexities has fascinated us. Join me as we pursue this dream by designing and developing a system worthy of the title "Artificial Intelligence."

CNC Router

Want to create a 3 axis computer controlled router, using stepper motors, and glider rails? Join our team as we engineer a device that can carve out your name onto a piece of wood!
