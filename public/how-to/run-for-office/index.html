How To Run For CSE Club Office

  1. Decide what position to run for, out of:
    • President
    • Vice President
    • Treasurer
    • Secretary
    • Event Coordinator
    • Information Officer
    • Webmaster
  2. Email president@cse-club.com, vice-president@cse-club, or treasurer@cse-club.com with:
    • The position(s) you want to run for
    • Your name
    • Your Coyote ID
    • A max 250-word personal statement
  3. Don't forget to be at the May 6th and 7th meetings.
  4. Participate in the vote from the evening of the 7th to the evening of the 11th.
