The CSE Club: 3 in 1

CSE Club @ CSUSB

The Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Club at CSUSB is a club for students with an interest in computer science, engineering, systems, and bioinformatics. We work to provide educational and professional opportunities for students in computing and engineering fields, including free tutoring, an expansive library of textbooks, and regular workshops. Our members are often found in our club room, JB 357; feel free to come by!

  • CSUSB IEEE Student Branch

    The CSE Club is CSUSB's Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Student Branch. As an IEEE Student Branch, the CSE Club regularly participates in the IEEE Region 6 Foothill Section's activities and promotes professional growth and networking between our members and the IEEE community.

  • CSUSB ACM Student Chapter

    The CSE Club is CSUSB's Association for Computing Machinery's Student Chapter. We actively report our activities to ACM and promote participation in ACM-hosted activities such as competitive programming and professional outreach.
