Computer Science & Engineering Club is a club at CSU, San Bernardino devoted to computer science, computer engineering, and computer systems. Interested in us? Feel free to checkout our Facebook Group or our Google Group or GitHub account. We also have a page on the CSE Department website. The club office is in Jack Brown room 540.

Membership Application

Our club is open to everyone, and we encourage you to join! You can do so by filling out the membership application and either turning it into an officer, or dropping it off in our office. We do have a membership fee of $15/quarter or $35/year. At this time we can only accept either cash or check.

Projects

We have a collection of projects for any members of the club to participate in. These are great opportunities to build skills, build your resume, and learn more about computer science and engineering. The current projects are:

  • Quadrotor Drone : This is a project to build a quadrotor that can ascend high into the sky and identify human forms.
  • Department of Defense Robotics Challenge : This is a long-term project from the DOD to develop a disaster-response robot that can handle tools, interact with humans, and navigate the difficult post-disaster environments.
  • CSE Club Website Development : This is a project to develop a brand new website for CSE Club. The website will be the central club hub, and will feature things like book checkout, membership management, events, and projects.
