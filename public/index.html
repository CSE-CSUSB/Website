Computer Science & Engineering Club is a club at CSU, San Bernardino devoted to computer science, computer engineering, and computer systems. Interested in us? Feel free to checkout our Facebook Group, Google Group, or GitHub account. We also have a page on the CSE Department website. The club office is in Jack Brown room 540.

Membership Application

Our club is open to everyone, and we encourage you to join! You can do so by filling out the membership application and either turning it into an officer, or dropping it off in our office. We do have a membership fee of $10 per quarter. At this time we can only accept either cash or check.

Calendar

Make sure to check out the complete official calendar from time to time to keep up with everything the club is doing.

Meetings

Every week, we hold a brief meeting followed by a workshop, where you can improve your skills and learn new things. Unless otherwise announced, our meetings and workshops are held:

To see our workshop schedule and past workshop documents, visit our workshops page. If you have an idea for a workshop, please let us know!

Projects

As a member of the CSE Club, you are able to participate in any of the five club projects we have. Participating in these projects is a great way to broaden and improve your abilities in our field. One of the most important things that you can have on your resume is a portfolio of work that you have done outside of university classes.

For more information on each of our individual projects, see our projects page.
