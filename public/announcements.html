  • IEEE Rising Stars Conference 2017 Contest

    Deadline: 05 Nov 2016 @ 11:59pm

    As the premier student event within IEEE Region 6, the Rising Stars conference brings together the most promising students and young professionals from within the region to network and be inspired by each other and the top tech companies from around the world. It will be taking place the first week in January 2017 at the Excalibur in Las Vegas, Nevada. This conference is in conjunction with the Consumer Electronics Showcase which occurs just after the conference.

    Rules:

    • Upload a video (up to 1min) to YouTube or write a paragraph (up to 700 char) telling us why you deserve to represent the CSUSB IEEE Student Branch at the 2017 IEEE Rising Stars Conference.

    • To enter, send an email to events.cse.csusb@gmail.com with the subject line: "IEEE Rising Stars". Be sure to include your name, coyote ID, IEEE member number, and your video link or paragraph.

    Eligibility:

    • Must be a student at CSUSB

    • Must be an IEEE Student Member. (Visit ieee.org)

    • Must be able to attend the event (Jan 2nd - 4th)

    • Must attend the IEEE Foothill Section meeting (Jan 10, 2017) to talk about the experience.
